Passphrase Requirements

  • A passphrase must be at least 12 characters in length.
  • A passphrase should contain at least one character from each of the following sets:
    • Uppercase alphabet (A-Z)
    • Lowercase alphabet (a-z)
    • Number (0-9) and special characters (such as # @ $ & among others)
  • A passphrase may not begin or end with the space character.
  • A passphrase may not contain more than two consecutive identical characters.
  • Passphrases should not contain: carriage return, linefeed, /, \, or a trailing * symbol.
