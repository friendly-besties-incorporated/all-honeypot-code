Swipe Card Management System (SCMS)

Log In

What is the SCMS?

  • Manages all swipe card terminals across all UMD facilities (i.e. buildings, offices, elevators, etc.).
  • Selectively grant/revoke student and faculty swipe access to facilities.
  • View all swipe card activity of a requested individual.
  • Access all attempted/successful swipe card authentications per location.
